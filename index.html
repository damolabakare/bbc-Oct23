HOW TO BUILD A COMPUTER

  1. Plan the purpose of your computer
  2. Decide how much you want to spend
  3. Research and buy the parts [hardware and software] you need
  4. Assemble the computer parts by following their included instructions
  5. Install your operating system software
  6. Update your hardware firmware
  7. Update your operating system software
  8. Install your other required software
  9. Update your software
  10. Enjoy your new computer!
